About Me

Hi, I'm Parker Slaughter. I'm an aspiring artist and cosplayer from Wichita, Kansas.

My favorite things to do include drawing and sketching in my sketchbook, singing in the car, and spending time with my boyfriend. I also love watching horror games, my favorite genre being analog horror.

I have only recently started digital art so I'm trying new techniques all the time! I mostly draw characters in an anime style as that is how I started drawing, also drawing lots of eyes.

Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Bars

Home / Projects / Treats / Recipe

This is a recipe I found on YouTube and I thought it looked amazing! Made with loads of chocolate chips and Heath candy bar pieces. Just good enough to share it with you and not keep it all to myself. Just good enough-! You'll make your coworkers and family members jealous for not having their hands on this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 170 grams of unsalted butter
  • 133 grams of granulated sugar
  • 200 grams of light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • Baking paper or PAM
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla bean paste
  • 270 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Heaping handful of chocolate chips (dark or milk chocolate)
  • Heaping handful of Heath bar bits
  • (Optional) Hershey's chocolate bar

Step by Step

  1. Brown unsalted butter in a pot.
  2. Pour browned, melted butter into a bowl and add in the granulated sugar and light brown sugar.
  3. Mix them together slowly. A spatula would work best.
  4. Add in the eggs and vanilla bean paste and whisk the mixture together.
  5. Add in the dry ingredients, the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Mix the dry and wet ingredients until almost combined.
  7. Add in most chocolate chips and the Heath bar bits. Fold them into the batter.
  8. Line the pan with baking paper or spray the pan with PAM.
  9. Transfer the dough into an 8x8 pan and spread it out evenly.
  10. Sprinkle the rest of the chocolate chips and Heath bar pieces over the top of the dough.
  11. (Optional Step) Add pieces of a Hershey's chocolate bar evenly spaced out on top of the dough.
  12. Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes.
  13. ENJOY!

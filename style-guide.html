My Colors

Headings and Navigation

Primary: #6a3436

This is primarily used for headings and navigation. This deep maroon can also be used for any bolding or the background colors of buttons.

Backgrounds

Secondary: #b4b4b4

The dark grey will mostly be used for backgrounds. Usually, content will be underlaid in white but if text ever needs to be used directly on it, color should be #ffffffcc.

Accents: #A29061, #104B6E, #060707

Here are the available accent colors. These can be used for side bars or background when the primary and secondary colors won’t work. These can also be used for links, buttons, hover effects, etc.
